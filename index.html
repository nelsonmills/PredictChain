PredictChain

Goals and Motivations

  • Decentralized prediction market on the Solana blockchain
  • Make predictions about everyday phenomena like the weather, music popularity, etc.
  • Use Solana tokens on the developer blockchain to bet on user beliefs
  • Provide a way to bet on common occurrences that are not traditionally bet on
  • Test whether incurred risk increases accuracy of forecasts
  • Appeals to users with an interest in cryptocurrency and prediction staking in everyday phenomena
  • No Solana-based prediction markets currently exist
  • Solana has fast and cheap transactions compared to other cryptocurrencies
  • A safe and legal platform for users to stake their predictions

Related Apps

  • Augur, Polymarket both prediction markets on Ethereum blockchain
  • PredictIt: centralized politics prediction market for research purposes

Approach

  • Solana smart contracts written in Rust
  • React frontend
  • Node backend with Express
  • MongoDB database
  • External Data Sources
    • Weather Data: OpenWeather API
    • Music Data: Spotify API

User Experience

  1. a user comes to our website
  2. they press a button and enter information to connect their crypto wallet
  3. they see a list of events, like weather events, that they can bet on
  4. they can search for certain events that interest them
  5. they have the option to create a betting event that all users can bet on
  6. whenever a user makes a bet, a solana smart contract is created for it
  7. when the expiration date of the bet comes, the bet is resolved, and the solana is distributed accordingly
  8. users can also buy and sell existing bets
  9. users can view their portfolio of bets
